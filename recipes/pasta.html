Indian style Pasta

Description

This is a basic pasta recipe prepared in the indian style(mainly cause I didn't have any cheese or butter or meat with me) It only requires a few veggies and chilli and turmeric powder and garam masala to make

Ingredients

  • Pasta - 200g
  • Onions - 1
  • Tomatoe - 1
  • Carrots - 1
  • Garlic - 1 full clove
  • Ginger - A small piece would do
  • Spices - 1/2 tablesppon of all except pepper
    1. Chilly powder
    2. Turmeric powder
    3. Garam Masala
    4. Coriander powder
    5. Ground pepper - 1 tablespoon
  • ghee - 1 tablespoon
  • plain flour - 1/4th of a cup or as required
  • milk - as required
  • ketchup - as required
  • salt - as required

Steps

  1. Prepare the vegetables

    2. chop the onions, carrots, tomatoes and garlic into fine blocks

  2. Sautee the vegitables

    3. Heat a pan and add oil to it, then add the onions and garlic int it

    Once the onions look golden-ish, add the tomatoes into it

  3. Boil the pasta

    4. for 200g pasta, you will most likely need 2 cups of water add 2 teaspoons of salt to the water and boil it, one the water is boiling, add the pasta and keep stirring every now and then. Drain the pasta(we'll need the pasta water later, don't pour it away) and keep it aside

  4. Add the spices

    5. The tomatoes should've melted by now, add the carrots and the Ginger after a bit, add 1/2 a tablespoon of all the spices into this. Add the pasta water to it and keep stirring under low flame

  5. Prepare the cream

    6. Add a tablespoon of ghee onto a pan and start heating it, add flour as required until it looks ground and clumpy, make sure not to burn the ghee, add milk as required after this, the ground matter should mix in fully with the milk, add the rest 1/2 tablespoon pepper to this.

    Notes - You can add some garlic to it if you want

  6. Finishing

    7. Add the cream to the veggies, then a bit of ketchup(if you want) Then add the pasta to this and stir until you get the above result
